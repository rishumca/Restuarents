A burger is a type of food that typically consists of a ground beef patty that is cooked, seasoned, and served between two slices of a bun. Burgers are often accompanied by toppings such as lettuce, tomato, cheese, pickles, onions, ketchup, and mustard. They are a popular fast food item and are also commonly served in restaurants and at backyard barbecues. In addition to beef, burgers can also be made with other meats such as chicken, turkey, or veggie patties for those who follow a vegetarian or vegan diet.
